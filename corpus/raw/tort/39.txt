During a neighborhood dispute, Tom accuses his neighbor, Jerry, of theft without any evidence, spreading the accusation among other residents. This leads to Jerry being ostracized by the community. In retaliation, Tom installs a powerful floodlight aimed directly at Jerry's bedroom window, causing a constant disturbance. Jerry confronts Tom, who responds by threatening physical harm if Jerry complains again. Feeling threatened and humiliated, Jerry considers legal action. Advise him.
