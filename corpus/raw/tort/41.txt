During a heated argument at a bar, Alex falsely accuses Sam of being a convicted criminal, loudly enough for other patrons to hear. Sam, feeling humiliated, attempts to leave, but Alex blocks the exit and threatens physical harm if Sam tries to leave. In the ensuing confrontation, Alex pushes Sam, causing him to fall and sustain injuries. Sam later develops PTSD, a recognised psychiatric injury. Sam later files a lawsuit against Alex. Advise him.
